Home

I'm interested in Machine Learning and Computer Vision (CV), on new technologies or applications. Currently, I'm working on explainable AI on image recognition; although I'm interested in foundational tasks as well as Large Language Models (LLMs) and state of the art like the transformer family of methods and most recent, MAMBA.
I'm currently finishing my PhD at École Centrale Marseille on the research group QARMA, under the tutelage of Ronan Sicre, Stéphane Ayache and Yannis Avrithis, on the topic of Explainable Image Recognition models.
I intend to continue researching in the area and shine light on the black-box behavior of deep learning models, enabling the design of better architectures, and allowing fairness and accountability of decisions.

About

I'm a Colombian Biomedical Engineer doing my Masters' Degree at Universidad de Los Andes, Colombia. I'm 25 years old, passionate about music, history and astronomy. During my undergraduate I grew passion towards programming and since then I've been working on Computer Vision under the supervission of my advisor Pablo Arbeláez. I love jogging, spending time looking at the sky and listening to music; specially if I can reproduce it when playing my guitar or bass. Whenever I have the means to, I also build scale models of planes of the WWII era.
